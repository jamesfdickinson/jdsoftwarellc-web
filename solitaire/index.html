Double Solitaire JD

Play different skill levels where all games are solvable. A solvable deal does not mean it will be easy. Earn achievements as you play. Earn up to 5 stars for a perfect game (no undo’s, 3 card draw, etc…). Replay previous levels to master a difficult deal. Track your progress down to a particular deal.

Speed is a game for two players of the shedding family of card games, in which each player tries to get rid of all of his cards. Speed is a game associated with the game Nertz.

Features

  • Live Online 2 player mode
  • Different skill levels
  • All Game solvable
  • Achievements
  • Undo
  • Drag or Smart Tap to move cards
  • 1 or 3 Card draw
  • Auto complete
