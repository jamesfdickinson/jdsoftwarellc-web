College Combat

Battle your friends in a hand to hand combat. College Combat is Mortal Kombat Clone and the characters filmed with a green screen. Each person that was lucky (or unlucky) enough to walk in my apartment while I had the green screen ready filmed and added to the battle arena as a digital fighter.

Eight player to choose from. Each have 3 unique move, 1 taunts, 3 ending animations.
